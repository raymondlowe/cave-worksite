Frequently Asked Questions - Melissani Cave

1. What is the best time to visit?
   Midday (11am-1pm) when sunlight enters the cave opening.

2. How much does entry cost?
   Adults: €7, Children: €4 (2025 prices)

3. Are photos allowed?
   Yes, photography is permitted but no flash.

4. Is swimming allowed?
   No, swimming is strictly prohibited.

5. How long is the boat tour?
   Approximately 15 minutes.

6. Is the cave wheelchair accessible?
   Partial access - the viewing platform is accessible.

7. What should I wear?
   Comfortable shoes and light jacket (cave is cool).

8. Are there restrooms?
   Yes, near the entrance.

9. Can I bring food/drinks?
   Only bottled water is allowed inside.

10. Is there parking?
    Yes, free parking available near the entrance.
