# Melissani Cave Facts

## Geological Facts
- Formed approximately 20,000 years ago
- Cave length: 150 meters
- Lake length: 100 meters
- Maximum depth: 30 meters
- Water is brackish (mix of fresh and sea water)

## Historical Facts
- Discovered in 1951
- Opened to public in 1963
- Named after the nymph Melissanthi from Greek mythology

## Visitor Facts
- Average annual visitors: 300,000
- Best visiting time: 11am-1pm (sunlight through opening)
- Boat tours last approximately 15 minutes
